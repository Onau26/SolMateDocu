### Description

The crimp-on contacts of the Molex Megafit connection at the input of the MPPT PCBA are visibly charred and therefore feature a very high contact resistance. This usually results in MPPT overcurrent faults that need to be cleared manually once they become permanent (3 cleared faults after another).
